differentia\\ is a modular research collective.
We sketch portraits of science and humanities using media arts.
We strive for exceptional storytelling. We believe in its power to effectively communicate scientific research & cultural concepts and mobilize resources to address pressing global challenges.
In conversations with artists, scientists and innovators, we glance at the future through the lens that researchers and practitioners across disciplines create with passion, conviction, and mastery of their field.
We aim to explore solutions to challenges affecting the future of Humanity that remain unsolved today.
