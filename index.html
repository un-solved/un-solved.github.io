Unsolved is a research collective.
We sketch portraits of science and humanities using media arts.
We strive for exceptional storytelling.
We communicate scientific research & cultural concepts to mobilize resources for pressing global challenges.
In conversations with scientists, innovators, and policy makers, we glance at the future through the lens that researchers and practitioners across disciplines create with passion, conviction, and mastery of their field.
We aim to explore solutions to currently overlooked challenges affecting our future that remain unsolved today.
